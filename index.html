When I Help

For fintechs expanding into Africa — in four situations.

You're entering a market you don't know
Which country first, which regulators matter, who the real competitors are, and what pricing actually works locally — the cost of guessing wrong is measured in licenses delayed and budgets burned.
You don't know who to partner with
Central banks, mobile money operators, aggregators and FX providers each move on their own logic. I've already built these relationships — with Ripple, Flutterwave, Binance and leading regional banks.
You need a team before you need a hire
Sales, operations, compliance and country finance — recruited, trained and managed, without a 6-month executive search or the risk of hiring the wrong person permanently.
You've launched, but growth has stalled
Operations run, but revenue isn't compounding. Someone senior needs to reset commercial priorities, renegotiate partnerships, and rebuild the growth plan — before more budget goes against the wrong channels.

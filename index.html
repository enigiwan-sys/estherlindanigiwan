01 — The Leader

A decade of building digital financial services that reach the people others overlook.

Esther Linda Nigiwan is a strategic fintech executive with over 10 years of experience leading market expansion, product commercialisation and revenue growth across digital financial services in Sub-Saharan Africa.

Currently serving as Head of Africa at Pyypl, she drives multi-country expansion strategy, owns regional P&L performance and delivers measurable transaction growth across eight African countries — Uganda, Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, Nigeria, Ghana, Mozambique and Sierra Leone.

Esther combines commercial and financial acumen with operational discipline, translating corridor behaviour analytics and customer insights into decisive go-to-market strategy. She has built and led a hybrid team of 70+ across multiple jurisdictions and structured high-impact partnerships with Ripple, Flutterwave and Binance.
